Swurf Savings

Swurf Savings is a tool designed for rideshare drivers to help manage saving more easily. Having been a Door Dash driver on the side for around 4 years, I have always wanted a clearer picture of how much I need to work to save up for my various savings goals. The nature of a rideshare job is that your earnings are at the whim of various different factors; this makes it difficult to plan savings goals accordingly. Swurf allows you to create new goals, and toggle whether they are active or not at any given time. When you add a new shift to the tracker, it suggests how much of each shift should be put away to each goal. This is a live project, and I am constantly adding new features to make the site more useful.

The codebase is split into two repositories, one for the front-end and one for the back-end. A demo can be viewed here. If you wish to use a demo account rather than create your own, the email is: testing@swurf.io, password: password123
